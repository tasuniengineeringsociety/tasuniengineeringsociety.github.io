Who We Are

About TUES

The Tasmanian University Engineering Society (TUES) is a student-led organisation representing students at the University of Tasmania. While centred around engineering, our society is open to all UTAS students, allowing us to engage a broad and diverse student community.

We are one of the most active societies on campus, delivering a wide range of events across the year — from large-scale social events to industry engagement opportunities and professional development initiatives.

In 2025, TUES was recognised as one of the largest societies on campus, hosting several of the university's biggest events including Engi Laneway, which attracts up to 1,000 attendees.

Our goal is to create a strong student community while connecting students with industry and new experiences both on and off campus.

Our Events

From massive festivals and formals to pub nights and sport — there's always something happening.

Flagship

Engi Laneway Music & Arts Festival

Our biggest event of the year — live bands, DJs, food and an incredible atmosphere with up to 1,000 students.

Social

Pub Nights

Regular nights out at local venues — great drinks, good company, and the perfect mid-semester wind-down.

Collab

Suits, Scrubs & Spanners

A massive collaborative party with the Law and Medical student societies. Three faculties, one epic night.

Formal

Engineering Ball

Our annual formal event — dress up, celebrate the year's achievements, and dance the night away.

Industry

Networking & Industry Panels

Connect with industry professionals through panels, networking nights, workshops and site visits.

Sport

Sport

Inter-faculty AFL, dodgeball, lawn bowls and more — get off campus and get moving with the team.

Community

Women in Engineering

Morning teas, networking events, and community-building initiatives supporting women in engineering.
